Our Mission

Laying the foundation for the next 100 years of food systems

Agriculture is at a turning point, with less than two decades to transform global food production, distribution, and consumption.

The Institute for Digital Agriculture is an interdisciplinary group of scientists, engineers, and agriculturalists working at the intersection of modern computing and modern agriculture. We are building an achitecture for Digital Agriculture that:

  1. Increases the adaptability of digital agricultural systems.
  2. Reduces software and hardware lock-in.
  3. Enables a full exploration of all space-time configurations of agricultural ecosystems and their costs and benefits.

Institute for Digital Agriculture is an independent organization based in California, USA. Contact us at info@digitalaginstitute.org.
