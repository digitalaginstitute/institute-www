Our Mission

Laying the foundation for the next 100 years of food systems

Civilization is at a turning point, with less than two decades to uproot and transform all global food production. We must act, but current science and technology is not up to the challenge.


The Institute for Digital Agriculture is an interdisciplinary group of scientists, engineers, and farmers working at the intersection of modern computing and modern agriculture. We are building an architecture for Digital Agriculture that enables a full exploration of space-time configurations of global agroecosystems with any crops, any management, and any environment: an all encompassing framework that contains all agriculture both past and future, and will enable the creation of hyperlocal, sustainable, and productive food systems.
